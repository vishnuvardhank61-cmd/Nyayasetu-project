Local Police Station (FIR)

For **Cognizable Offenses**—crimes where police can arrest without a warrant.

When to go here:
  • Physical Assault or Threats
  • Theft, Robbery or Burglary
  • Severe Domestic Violence
  • Accidents or Hit-and-Run
Pro Tip:

If they refuse to file an FIR, you can send it via post to the Superintendent (SP) or approach a Magistrate under Section 156(3) CrPC.
